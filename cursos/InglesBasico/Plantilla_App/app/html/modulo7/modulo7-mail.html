The following letter is an example of one type of letter you may receive by mail. This type of correspondence is typical of a letter you may receive asking you to purchase something. You do NOT have to reply to this type of letter if you are not interested.

La siguiente carta es un ejemplo de carta que puede recibir por correo. Este tipo de correspondencia es típica de una carta que puede recibir en la cual le piden que compre algo. No tiene que responder a este tipo de carta si no está interesado.

Dear Mr. Jones,

My name is Jeff Bridges. I deliver the newspaper in your neighborhood. I would like to know if you would like me to deliver the newspaper to your house every day for the low price of $40.00 a month. If you are interested, please call me at 123-4567.

Thank you,

Jeff Bridges
